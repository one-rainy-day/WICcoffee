About us


Freshly roasted coffee and nature-inpired meal


WIC Coffee’s inception was a childhood outdoor adventure amongst grandma's many fruit trees including the big-leafed and lofty liberica coffee. The cherries’ hues of red, the otherworldly complexion of the green beans, and the aroma of the roast are to be recalled much later through an espresso machine, a grinder, a modified popcorn machine, and an insatiable desire to make better coffee.
